Good design is not just seen, it's experienced.

Our design approach is holistic and collaborative. We don't think of design as window dressing or decoration, we think of it as the thoughtful creation of an application as a complete system.

That's why we offer a full range of services in design, technology and project management to industry leaders who want to use technology to expand their business. Incorporating business, human and technical factors ensures that your vision is carried from project inception through implementation. Experienced designers and technologists work together with a shared understanding and purpose: to create an application that is highly functional, so that the user's experience with it is logical, intuitive and pleasurable.

Services

User Experience

User experience involves every visible point of contact with your audience. The user experience team ensures that the visual design, information and content components are on- brand and synchronized with offline initiatives, to meet and exceed audience expectations and desires. It includes:

  • Creative direction
  • Information architecture
  • Content strategy
  • Copywriting
  • Visual design
  • Brand strategy
  • Visual quality assurance
Technical Expertise

We develop on a range of platforms, depending on your project requirements, delivering the most appropriate technology solution for your needs. The work is fully documented in project specifications and diagrams, and created using open source software and programming languages to ensure that the system grows with your business. Specific services are:

  • Technical research
  • Requirements analysis
  • System architecture and design
  • System integration
  • Implementation (presentation, logic and data layer)
  • Quality assurance testing
