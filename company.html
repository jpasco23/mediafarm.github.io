Who We Are

Media Farm was founded in 1995 by Daniel Scheman Neu, who graduated from New York University's Interactive Telecommunications Program and, with an interest in user-centered design, sought to apply its principles to emerging technology to create systems that improve operations and create new models for doing business.

The Media Farm team is made up of three distinct disciplines in design, technology and project management. Each member comes from a multidisciplinary background, with an ability to think on many different levels to see the big picture. Every employee at Media Farm is smart, hard-working and experienced, and able to listen and adapt to change. This is a key benefit because every project is a custom solution, serving a very specific set of needs for each client. That blend of experience and agility is what makes projects succeed.
What We Do

We design and develop Web-enabled technology systems for businesses that want to communicate effectively with their audience, increase sales or streamline business operations. These systems include:
  • Websites
  • Content management systems
  • Customer communication systems
  • Marketing applications

While we have extensive expertise in design and technology, our shared focus is on people and how they use what we build. This is the cornerstone of user-centered design.
How We Work

We work collaboratively with our clients, partnering our design and technology expertise with their vision to create systems that reinvigorate businesses and help them thrive. Since it's a new, and fairly complex medium, we educate our clients to help them understand how the application works, because we believe that having the right information empowers confident decisions.
